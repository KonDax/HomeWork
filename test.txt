My family
My name is Kate. I am from Canada. I am 13
years old. I have got a big family: my mother
Jane, my father Oliver, my brother Tom, my
sister Emily. My father works as an engineer.
My mother is a teacher. She teaches French for
students at school. My elder sister works as a
waitress in a cafe. My younger brother is a
pupil in a school. We are a friendly family.
